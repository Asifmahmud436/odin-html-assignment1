Omlete

Ingrediants:

  • Oil
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • An egg

Steps:

  1. Use two or three eggs per omelette, depending on how hungry you are.
  2. Use an 8-inch nonstick skillet for a 2-egg omelette, a 9-inch skillet for 3 eggs
  3. Let the eggs sit for a minute, then use a heatproof silicone spatula to gently lift the cooked eggs from the edges of the pan.
  4. Add the filling—but don’t overstuff the omelette—when the eggs begin to set. Cook for a few more seconds
  5. Fold the omelette in half. Slide it onto a plate with the help of a silicone spatula.
