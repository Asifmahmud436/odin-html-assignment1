French Fries

Ingredients:

  • Oil
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • 3-4 Potatoes

Steps:

  1. Cut fries with a serrated knife (secret crispiness tip #1)
  2. No soaking, just rinse
  3. Gently simmer 10 minutes in vinegar water (secret crispiness tip #2! And no, you cant taste vinegar)
  4. Shallow-fry twice
